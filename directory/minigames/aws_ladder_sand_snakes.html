🎲 Snakes & Ladders – Instructions

🎯 Objective

Reach square 60 first to win the game 🎉

▶️ How to Play

  1. Click Roll Dice
  2. Move automatically across the board
  3. Answer questions after each turn

🐍 Snakes

Landing on a snake moves you down.

🪜 Ladders

Landing on a ladder moves you up.

⚠️ Rules

  • Max square is 60
  • You must answer questions to continue

Click roll to start!
